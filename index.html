Chizzy charity foundation aims to make a positive impact on people's lives by supporting causes that matter. We are committed to improving communities and helping those in need. Join us in making a difference. Together, we can create a better world. CNF is a Global nonprofit, non-governmental organization registered in Nigeria, to implement humanitarian relief and long term development projects, educational and health programs, and human rights programs in crisis regions internationally. Its director is Sir Charles Nnamchi
Our charity foundation was established with a mission to improve lives and communities. We believe in making a positive impact by supporting causes that matter. Our focus is to provide aid to those in need and help build a better future. Your support will help us achieve our goal and make a tangible difference in people's lives. We invite you to be a part of this meaningful journey with us.Our charity foundation's mission is to improve the lives of underprivileged individuals and communities through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. We believe that by providing access to these essential resources, we can break the cycle of poverty and create a brighter future for those in need. Join us in our efforts to make a positive impact on the world.
Our charity foundation's vision is a world where every person has the opportunity to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or circumstances. We strive to create a more equitable society by addressing the root causes of poverty and inequality through innovative and sustainable solutions. Join us in our pursuit of a brighter future for all.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

Our charity foundation is dedicated to providing healthy food to those in need. We understand the importance of access to nutritious meals and the impact it can have on overall health and well-being.

Pure Water

Our charity provides pure water to those in need for free of charge. Join us in making a positive impact on health and well-being in the community.

Health Care

Our charity offers accessible health care services to those in need, for free of charge. Join us in promoting health and wellness in the community.

Primary Education

Our charity provides quality primary education to underprivileged children for free. Invest in the future by supporting education and shaping young minds.

Residence Facilities

Our charity provides safe and affordable residence facilities to those in need, for free. Join us in creating a supportive community for those in need

Social Care

Our charity offers compassionate social care to those in need, for free. Join us in making a positive impact on the well-being and quality of life for those in our community
